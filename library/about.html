Learning Resource Center Information System

The Maryknoll College of Panabo, Inc. maintains a library and audio-visual that provides resources and services to support the MCPI community in their love for reading, search for information, and research.
The Library serves the students, teaching, non-teaching personnel, and administrators both for their leisure reading, instruction, and researches. The library also offers its services to various individuals (e.g., alumni, visitors, government workers) outside the campus provided that they seek the approval of the president of the school to use the library as a venue for their researches. An open-shelf system is used by the library to provide greater access to the collection. The print and non-print materials are being catalogued and classified according to the Dewey Decimal Classification System.
