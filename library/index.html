Readers services

The library readers services provide assistance to the library users for effective, efficient and easier access and retrieval of library materials from Learning Resource Center.

Bibliographic Service

We offer bibliographic services related to the library collection and access to those collections, whether print or online. The LRC can help assist students and faculty with regards to bibliography making using APA format.

Current Awareness Service

The MCPI-LRC updates students and faculty about free access online resources coming from different websites or public library access. It also provides information to users on the newly acquired database, library materials and updates school activities.

Library Instruction/Orientation Program

MCPI-LRC librarians from IBED and College conduct orientation to students and faculty in the beginning of school year and during the start of classes. Then, it follows up the students in a monthly/quarterly basis during Library Instruction/Library period.

Database Assistance

MCPI-LRC library personnel assist and conduct training to students and teachers on the utilization of the database program of the school to ensure that everyone will know how to access the database in the online platform. MCPI-LRC also provides assistance to students and faculty who have the difficulty in navigating the said database being subscribed.

Online Collection of Electronic Library (E-Library)

Maryknoll College of Panabo, Inc. subscribes to digital and online collection to be used by the students/pupils during this pandemic time to ensure the delivery of reading materials is still consistent even if the students/pupils are at home. The students/pupils can still read, do researches and have leisure reading plus funfilled activities provided by the databases that are being subscribed by the school and the learning resource center.

In partnership with the Gale in Context: Elementary and World book e-book students/pupils can still access with library online collections at the comfort of their homes for both modular and online learning modalities.

ABOUT WORLDBOOK EBOOK

• World Book eBooks contain something for all ages and all subjects. This digital library includes highly illustrated, engaging titles that span the curriculum and reading levels. Some books include interactive games, quizzes, and activities to enrich learning. A subscription includes automatic updates and revision to eBooks, as well as any new titles that are added to the site.

About GALE in Context Elementary

Gale In Context: Elementary is a simple, cross-curricular resource that ensures equity and continuity for all students. As part of the Gale In Context suite of resources, this database advances education and gives children the research skills they will need in middle school, high school, and beyond.
