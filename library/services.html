Readers services

The library readers services provide assistance to the library users for effective, efficient and easier access and retrieval of library materials from Learning Resource Center.

Bibliographic Service

We offer bibliographic services related to the library collection and access to those collections, whether print or online. The LRC can help assist students and faculty with regards to bibliography making using APA format.

Current Awareness Service

The MCPI-LRC updates students and faculty about free access online resources coming from different websites or public library access. It also provides information to users on the newly acquired database, library materials and updates school activities.

Library Instruction/Orientation Program

MCPI-LRC librarians from IBED and College conduct orientation to students and faculty in the beginning of school year and during the start of classes. Then, it follows up the students in a monthly/quarterly basis during Library Instruction/Library period.

DATABASE ASSISTANCE

MCPI-LRC library personnel assist and conduct training to students and teachers on the utilization of the database program of the school to ensure that everyone will know how to access the database in the online platform. MCPI-LRC also provides assistance to students and faculty who have the difficulty in navigating the said database being subscribed.

Mission

As a center of intellectual inquiry, provides resources and place for resourceful discussion , research and cultural activities that support the vision and mission of the Maryknoll College of Panabo, Inc and its curricular needs.

Vision

Visualizes as a primary center in the provision of instructional materials services using the best technology available.

Goal

Deliver excellent library and audio-visual services and provide the best possible resources on behalf of instruction and research. In pursuit of this goal, development efforts are made:
1.) Development of the library and audio-visual collection focusing on the curricular needs of the school and in the field of instruction
2.) Automation of the library services for the fast retrieval of information resources.

Objectives


1. Procure and organize print and non-print materials and other library resources to instruction and research in the evolving subject areas of the curriculum;
2. Provide easy access to collection and holdings;
3. Serve as a center of reliable information; and
4. Develop, enrich, update, and maintain the library and audio-visual collection to support the program offerings of the institution
5. Use technology to improve the delivery of services and resources
6. Enhance the information literacy of the library user to ensure greater access and utilization of the resources
7. Engage in staff development activities that will enable library staff to continuously improve services in an everchanging information environment
8. Develop library programs that uplift literacy and build learners reading culture in the community.
