    Frequently Asked Question

    In response to the country's situation amidst the COVID-19 pandemic, the MCPI has considered two modes of instruction delivery, the Online (Learning Management System) or Modular (Textbooks and Learning Guide). Each modality has its own strength in catering the diverse needs of the students. With this, students will be able to keep abreast with the frenetic changes in the educational landscape. In addition, MCPI strongly encourages the parents and guardians to take a part as full partners in constituting a wholesome learning experience in the safety and comfort of their homes. Kindly take a look at the pictures or click the link to fully understand the distinction of each modality.

    Will Textbooks and other supplies still be required for Online learning?

    Textbooks are required for online learning. Therefore students need to rent/purchase the books which serve as their learning materials.

    Will textbook and other supplies still be required for modular learning?

    Modular learning in our school requires both textbooks and learning guide. Therefore students need to rent/purchase the books which serve as their learning materials.

    WHAT ARE THE ADVANTAGES OF TAKING ONLINE CLASS?

    Online class offers good features for virtual discussions and interactive activities. It caters both synchronous and asynchronous mode of delivery. Also, it provides immediate feedback and direct follow-up activities for the students.

    WHAT ARE THE ADVANTAGES OF TAKING MODULAR CLASS?

    Modular class offers self-paced and asynchronous mode of delivery. The students under modular class will be given learning guides every week. Also, this type of modality needs direct supervision from the parents/guardians. (Learning guides will be delivered to a specific area of meetup in Barangay. Only parent/guardian is allowed to pick up the materials.)

    WHAT IF WE HAVE MORE THAN ONE CHILD ENROLLED IN ONLINE LEARNING? HOW WILL WE MANAGE THE SHARING OR USE OF GADGETS AND DEVICES?

    The school considers the number of children exposed in online learning thus, most of the activities will be delivered via “asynchronous” so students can accomplish activities at their own pace with friendly time-given by the teacher

    HOW WILL WE BE PREPARED ON THIS ONLINE CLASS? WHAT ARE THE MEASURES WE NEED TO UNDERGO?

    Both students and parents/guardians will undergo orientation before the start of the class. The school has already prepared schedules for orientation to parents/guardians.

    WHAT IF DURING VIRTUAL DISCUSSIONS, OUR CHILD WILL EXPERIENCE CONNECTIVY PROBLEM?

    The Learning Management System (LMS) provides a lot of friendly features that caters problem during sessions. One of its features is a recorded video which the teacher can do to address that problem. It can then be posted to its dashboard.
